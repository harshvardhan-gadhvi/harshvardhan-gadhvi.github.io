About

A writer who
also composes.

Harshvardhan Gadhvi is a writer, musician, and creative based in Ahmedabad, India. His fiction explores the inner lives of people who perform competence while quietly unravelling — characters who live publicly and grieve privately.

His debut novel follows Maya Calloway, an ethics columnist whose public persona is a contradiction of everything she privately feels. The story is a romance-mystery built around a dare that changes everything.

As a musician, he performs as Brahmasmi — singer, guitarist, producer, and lyricist, with music on Spotify, Apple Music, and SoundCloud. He is also the founder of Swar Sadhna School of Music in Morbi.

The novel

Untitled
Maya Calloway
Harshvardhan Gadhvi
Romance · Mystery · Literary Fiction

A woman who advises everyone on ethics — except herself.

Maya Calloway is an ethics columnist with a reputation built on clarity and principle. But when a ten-week dare forces her into a fake marriage with a man who is trying to leave, and a mystery that refuses to stay buried — she must confront the version of herself she has spent a lifetime hiding from.

This is a story about identity, self-suppression, and what it means to finally choose yourself.
